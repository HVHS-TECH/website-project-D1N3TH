University Entrance

What you actually need to get in to university...

Understanding how to get into university is essential for every student. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you meet the entrance requirements in New Zealand. To gain University Entrance you need:

  • NCEA Level 3
  • 14 credits in three approved subjects
  • 10 literacy credits (5 in reading, 5 in writing)
  • 10 numeracy credits
  • Some universities or specific programs (e.g. Medicine, Law, Engineering) have higher entry requirements or require extra assessments. Always check university websites for details.
