Scholarships

How they work and how to get one...

University can be expensive, but scholarships can help! Here’s what you need to know:

  1. Types of Scholarships
    • Academic scholarships - For high-achieving students
    • Sports and Cultural scholarships - For athletes or performers
    • Equity scholarships - For students from disadvantaged backgrounds
    • Maori and Pasifika scholarships - For eligible students
  2. Where to find scholarships?
    • Universities’ official websites
    • StudyLink (NZ Government)
    • Private organisations and companies
  3. Application tips
    • Start early - deadlines vary
    • Write a strong personal statement
    • Get recommendation letters
    • Meet all criteria before applying
