International Students

A guide for students coming from overseas, covering visa requirements, English language requirements, university application processes, and tips for adjusting to life in New Zealand

If you're coming to New Zealand from overseas, here’s what you need to know:

  1. Visa Requirements
    • Student Visa (Apply via Immigration NZ)
    • Proof of Funds (At least $20,000 per year for living expenses)
    • Health and Travel Insurance
  2. English Language Requirements
    Most universities require:
    • IELTS (6.0 - 6.5)
    • TOEFL or other recognised tests
  3. Adjusting to Life in NZ
    • Join student groups for support
    • Try local food and experience Kiwi culture
    • Learn about public transport options
