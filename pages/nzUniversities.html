NZ Universities

So where can I study in New Zealand?

  • University of Auckland - Largest university, strong in research
  • Victoria University of Wellington - Known for Law and Humanities
  • University of Canterbury - Strong in Engineering and Science
  • University of Otago - Famous for Health Sciences and Student Life
  • Massey University - Specialises in Distance Learning and Agriculture
  • Auckland University of Technology - Innovative and Modern
  • Lincoln University - Focused on Environment and Agribusiness
  • University of Waikato - Strengths in IT and business
